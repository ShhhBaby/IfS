Our Platform:

IfS is built from the ground up as a learning site for adults who are not experienced with the use of the latest technology on the internet. The platform comes with a dedicated page which consists of simple tutorials for each of the most useful internet technologies.




The main technologies that are used the most by senior citizens:


According to a survey analysis, senior citizens spend most of their time with YouTube as it provides a wide range of videos and is also very user friendly. There also seems to be a rise in the amount of senior citizens who want to be able to use social media effectively. We also have a dedicated page for major social media platforms like WhatAspp with information on how to use the application.
